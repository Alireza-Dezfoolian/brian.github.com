Here is my current list of cool OS X applications.

Meteorologist: free weather app

iStumbler: discover wireless networks and bluetooth devices

VooDooPad: hypertext notetaking fun

SubEthaEdit: very unique coding and writing app, meant to be used collaboratively through Rendezvous

OmniOutliner: first-class outliner, note-taking tool with a couple of bonus features thrown in (like columns) to make it addictively powerful

Very exciting news for everyone who has every coveted a Google Appliance: Google has announced a new edition for websites and small to mid-size companies. The Google Mini (no relation to the recently announced Mac Mini) will search up to 50,000 documents and costs $5000, as opposed to nearly $30K for the enterprise edition.

Read more about it on eWeek and CNET.

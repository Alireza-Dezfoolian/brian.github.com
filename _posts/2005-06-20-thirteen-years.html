Today, Lori and I celebrate our 13th wedding anniversary! We got married one week after graduating from Michigan State in 1992. We had actually started dating 5 years earlier, during our junior year of high school! Only God's grace could explain how I ended up with the most wonderful wife and mother in the world - I certainly don't deserve the privilege. I would count myself blessed just to know her, and somehow, I get to spend the rest of my life with her.

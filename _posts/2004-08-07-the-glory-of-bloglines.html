InfoWorld's Jon Udell evaluates Bloglines, a free web-based RSS newsreader, and comes to the conclusion that it's nearly perfect. I know many people who use Bloglines and have experimented with it myself. I especially recommend it to those who would like to experiment with weblogs, as it is currently free and accessible from any computer, so you don't have to install any software.

If you want better speed and a few more features, and are willing to part with $40, I recommend Radio Userland (Mac or PC) and NetNewsWire (Mac).

Burning Door (the makers of FeedBurner, which provides this site's RSS feed) has released the second beta of RIM Blackberry Mobile Feed Reader, dangerously combining two 21st Century addictions > weblogs and the Blackberry. Yes, spouses across the country will be thrilled to hear that there is now one more reason for people to stare into their Blackberry's.

"Yes, honey, you look lovely tonight, but you don't understand, Scoble just linked to me!"

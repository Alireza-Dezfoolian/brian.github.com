Frank Luntz on Ron Paul

His supporters are the equivalent of crabgrass. It's not the grass you want, and it spreads faster than the real stuff. They just like him because he's the most anti-Establishment of all the candidates, the most likely to look at the camera during the debates and say, 'Hey, Washington, f____ you.'

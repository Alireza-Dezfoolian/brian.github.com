The next stop on the book journey is North Carolina, where the book actually attended its first college basketball game! The Blogging Church landed in the hands of church planter Tadd Grandstaff - check out the great picture and review on his blog.

Brian and Terry do a great job of laying out why you should blog, what the purpose of blogging is, how to build a solid blog, podcasting, and everything else you have ever wanted to know about blogging. The book reads really easy and it is set-up so that each chapter is broken into multiple categories which allows you to skim through the chapters and read the sections that resonate with you the most.

Thanks, Tadd!

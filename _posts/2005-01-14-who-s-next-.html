After recently being chosen as a writer for the 100Bloggers project, I understand that one of my first tasks is to select the next participant. I have a few people in mind who I'll be contacting shortly, but if you're a blogger and are interested, feel free to drop me a note or leave a comment here. Let me know what you love to write about and what your perspective would bring to the project.

You can read more about the project and the initial set of participants from the man who started it all: Jon Strande.

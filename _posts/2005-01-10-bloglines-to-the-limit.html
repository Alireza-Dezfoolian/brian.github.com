Josh Bancroft at GeekBlog.org has posted a thorough list of reasons why he loves Bloglines, the very popular web-based weblog/RSS reader [via Scoble]. In the process, he points out a number of cutting-edge features that many people may not know about.

One cool feature is the ability to display your live, up-to-date Bloglines blogroll (subscription list) on a site through a single line of good.

One way cool feature is Bloglines Mobile. Enter a simple web address on any mobile phone, PDA, or Blackberry, login with your Bloglines account, and now you have access to your favorite weblogs anytime, anyplace. I've tested this and the site they built for mobile devices is quick, elegant, and easy to use. Wow!

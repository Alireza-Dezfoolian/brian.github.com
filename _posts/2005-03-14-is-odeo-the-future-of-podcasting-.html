--- layout: post title: Is Odeo the Future of Podcasting? published: 1 ---

I stopped by the Future of Podcasting session this afternoon at SXSW. Though the panel had a wide-range of experience and good information, I found the most interesting element was the sight of Evan Williams relaxing on a couch outside of the room. As a key player in the current podcasting company of-the-moment, Odeo, I find it equally fascinating that he wasn't interested in being there, and that someone from SXSW didn't make sure he was on the stage.

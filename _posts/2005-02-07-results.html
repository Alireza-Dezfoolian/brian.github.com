Terry Storch's 52 Leadership Tips keep getting better and better. This week's focus is Follow Through. Terry writes about how innovation and creativity must be matched with equal amounts of follow through. His post reminded me a bit of Apple before Steve Jobs returned. The company was nearly drowning in research projects and endless quests for the latest and greatest, but rarely executed the final, crucial step: delivery of a successful product.

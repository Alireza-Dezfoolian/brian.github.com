As I've been researching and purchasing equipment over the past two weeks, our new developer, Brad, has introduced me to a number of great hardware websites. We've found some incredible prices on these:

Ben's Bargains > excellent for finding the up-to-the-minute best deals on Dell hardware, among other things. We've used this site to save hundreds of dollars on recent Dell orders.

NewEgg > very good prices and reasonable shipping. We found terrific prices on Crucial memory and high-end hard drives.

Pricewatch > the best pricing data available on technology products.

Slick Deals > bargains on a wide-variety of products from a huge selection of internet retailers.

Warning: Some of these sites can become addictive!

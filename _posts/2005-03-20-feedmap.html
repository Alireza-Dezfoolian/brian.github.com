FeedMap is an interesting new way to connect weblogs with real life. Chandu Thota has written a cool web app that uses MapPoint to display blog locations on a map [via Scoble]. In other words, you can get a picture of who is blogging in your very own neighborhood. You'll find my experiment on the right-side of my blog.

Check out FeedMap.

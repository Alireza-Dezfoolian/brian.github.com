--- layout: post title: Playoff Baseball published: 1 ---

Tonight was our first official experience of playoff baseball, and a happy one at that as Ben's Angels won. If they win on Thursday night, they will play for the league championship.

The game was completely different than any of his games in the last 4 seasons. Ben said afterwards that it was the most exciting game he'd ever played. The coaches, players, and, yes, fans were so much more focused and enthusiastic. It was a close game until late and very tense.

Ben had 2 singles, scored a run, and had a few smart, hustle plays. All in all, very fun, but Thursday will be a challenge (for all of us!).

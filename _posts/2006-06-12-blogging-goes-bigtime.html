--- layout: post title: Blogging Goes Bigtime published: 1 ---

How do you know when blogging has arrived? When a blogger leaves a company and the news is worthy of the Wall Street Journal homepage. Word spread yesterday that Robert Scoble is leaving Microsoft for a position with a Silicon Valley start-up, PodTech.net. In fact, the story was ranked higher than a fresh wave of violence in Baghdad. The news is also in the print Journal this morning.

Congratulations, Robert, on your fresh start! It's great news that you'll be closer to your son, and I know you'll do incredible things at PodTech!

The Living Room Candidate is an innovative online museum exhibition presenting more than 250 political television ads from every presidential campaign since 1952. The site also includes a searchable database along with historical background and results of each election. There is also a physical exhibit that happens to be in Dallas at the 6th Floor Museum.

Backback from 37Signals launches tomorrow!

Here's the description from the site:

"Getting organized is easy. Gather your ideas, to-dos, notes, photos, and files online. Set email and mobile reminders so you don't forget the little things. Easily collaborate with others."

The pricing model is also listed (Free, $5, $9, $19/month), but it's not clear yet what you get at each level. Tomorrow should be fun!

Simple, focused software that does just what you need and nothing you don't. A terrific mission statement for all software development.

Update: Backpack is now live! You can start a page with a to-do list, then add notes, files, and images as the project builds.

An incredible week for U.S. Soccer just ended.

In the World Youth Championship (Under 20) today, the U.S. defeated Argentina 1-0.

In World Cup Qualifying, the Men's National Team defeated Costa Rica in Salt Lake City 3-0 and then Panama on the road five days later by the same score. Both matches featured incredible play by Kasey Keller and Landon Donovan. These victories almost guarantee that the U.S. will be playing in Germany next year, qualifying for the World Cup for the fifth straight time.

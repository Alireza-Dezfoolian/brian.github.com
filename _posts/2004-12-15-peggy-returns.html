--- layout: post title: Peggy Returns published: 1 ---

After taking a three month leave-of-absence from The Wall Street Journal, Peggy Noonan has returned to her weekly column. Peggy is head-and-shoulder above nearly every political writer and commentator and is well worth reading whenever possible. Begin with her post-election column, So Much to Savor, and continue through her most recent essay on Hilary Clinton' future.

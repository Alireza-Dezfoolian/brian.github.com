One month after the Fellowship Church Blog launch, the CP Blog is now live! The CP Blog is the official home for the latest and greatest from CreativePastors.com.

CreativePastors is the one-stop site for Ed Young and Fellowship Church resources. Thousands of messages, media materials, and downloads are available to help local churches reach their community.

The blog will feature posts from all of the talented people on the CP team and will provide a behind-the-scenes view, in addition to product information, how-to's, and feedback from people on the front lines of ministry.

Take a minute to check it out, subscribe, and say hello!

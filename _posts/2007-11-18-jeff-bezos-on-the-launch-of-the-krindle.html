--- layout: post title: Jeff Bezos on the launch of the Krindle published: 1 ---
This is the most important thing we've ever done. It's so ambitious to take something as highly evolved as the book and improve on it. And maybe even change the way people read.

Jeff Bezos on Amazon's new book reader, the Krindle. The device debuts tomorrow for $400. New books are $10 or less and it's connected to the web wirelessly for purchases and research. You can also subscribe to the WSJ and NYT, as well as magazines. 30 hours of battery life. Steven Levy of Newsweek has the full report. Exciting stuff. I can't wait to see the details tomorrow.

The Weekly Standard provides a great collection of debate analysis:

P.J. O'Rourke's Putting Words in the President's Mouth offers the President 16 pre-debate talking points. A must read!

Hugh Hewitt's A Pretty Good Hand focuses on where Bush stands following the final debate.

Jonathan Last's Return of the King gives excellent, hilarious, round-by-round coverage (worth it for the USA Today references alone).

Fred Barnes's On All Cyclinders calls Bush's performance his best as a candidate, likely to assure his re-election.

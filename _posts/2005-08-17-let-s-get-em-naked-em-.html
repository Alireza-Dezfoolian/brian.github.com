Shel Israel and Robert Scoble have completed their new book on business blogging, Naked Conversations! Congratulations to two terrific guys who have written what looks to be an outstanding book. Congratulations to Jim Minatel as well, the Wrox acquisitions editor who has played a big role in making this happen, and was the only one to have child in the middle of the process!

You can visit the book blog to find out more and read the chapter drafts. They've done a great job of writing the book in a very open and transparent way, including posting interview transcripts. If my small part survives the editor's pen, you'll find me in Chapter 5. I've learned a lot about blogging throughout the process and am eager to read the finished product.

The book is already on Amazon and is due out in January.

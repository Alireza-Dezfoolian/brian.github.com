Today is our 12th wedding anniversary. Since it also happens to be Father's Day, we celebrated yesterday with a great evening of dinner and a movie. We saw The Terminal, which was as good as Roger Ebert said it was.

Anyone who knows us both knows just how blessed I am. Lori is truly a phenomenal woman in every way, talented and creative, joyful and giving, loving and lovely. What a wonderful 12 years it has been.

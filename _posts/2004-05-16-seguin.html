After Ben's baseball game and recital on Saturday, we drove north of Denton for a dinner party at a friend's ranch. The primary excuse for sharing this piece of information is showing off two pictures I took of possibly the cutest little girl in the world, Seguin.

Seguin is three years old and Lori actually took care of her three days a week for the first year of her life. For Ben, she has always been the little sister he never had. Each time we visit, the highlight is seeing the two of them together. I have no doubt that Ben would have made a wonderful big brother. Her favorite games to play are chase and hide n' seek, and Ben does both with patience and enthusiasm.

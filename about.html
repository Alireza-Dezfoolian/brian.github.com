Hello, my name is Brian and I'm an author, product manager, and content strategist. I currently work with the talented team at Return Path. Before that, I was the Glue Guy at Gowalla, from our start above a Starbucks in a Dallas suburb, through our move to Austin and our acquisition by Facebook four years later. If you'd like to connect with me professionally, introduce yourself on LinkedIn.

A few years ago, I wrote a book on blogging that reached #3 on Amazon's Movers & Shakers list and #148 overall. I know this because I hit refresh often. It included contributions from Kathy Sierra, Guy Kawasaki, Merlin Mann, Dave Winer, Robert Scoble, David Weinberger, Shel Israel, and more. Seth Godin even provided a quote for the cover. I hope to write another one.

Some of my favorite things are chai lattes, Arsenal FC, hiking, Austin, presidential campaigns, listening to live music, writing, and chasing new ideas.

I met my wife, Lori, in 8th grade and I've been thanking God for her ever since. It's like Wonder Years if Kevin and Winnie had ended up together. We have a delightful, talented son, Ben, who is in high school, but still enjoys hanging out and going to Radiohead concerts with me.

I love sharing life with creative and curious people. I hope you’re one of them.

I’m @bb on Twitter. Say hello or drop me a note.
